Data Transparency 2017 (DT2017)

September 26, 2017 @ 8:00 am - 6:00 pm

  • « 2017 DATA Act Summit
  • Open Data Day 2018: »

Across government, compliance, and the private sector, open data is taking over and new technologies are taking hold. Join the Data Foundation on Tuesday, September 26th, to learn how.

We will be taking a deep dive into three areas: government data, compliance data, and private-sector data.

DT2017 will kick off with keynote addresses from Aneesh Chopra, former Chief Technology Officer of the United States and author of Innovative State, and Chris Liddell, Assistant to the President and White House Director of Strategic Initiatives. Live demonstrations from the U.S. Treasury Department of DATA Act analytics and from the Australian government of Standard Business Reporting will lead into program tracks on government data, compliance data, and private-sector data.
