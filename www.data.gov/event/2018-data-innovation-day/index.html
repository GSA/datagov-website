National Technical Information Service (NTIS) 2018 Data Innovation Day – Addressing National Priorities through Data Insights

From The NTIS Team:
Please join the Commerce Department’s NTIS for 2018 Data Innovation Day!
Participate in a lively discussion of how agencies are tackling national priorities, deriving data insights, and accelerating mission outcomes! See how federal agencies are leveraging NTIS’s joint venture authority to improve operational effectiveness and enhance citizen services. Hear how federal agencies are using evidence-based tools from data collection through analysis and application. Discover a compelling data innovation model that empowers agencies to accelerate their data-driven priorities, while transforming data from being a mere commodity to becoming a fully-realized strategic data asset.

We hope that you’re able to join the conversation involving data science innovation across the federal government!

Cheers,

The NTIS Team

Who should attend: Federal agency officials responsible for implementing data strategies, including Chief Data Officers and Chief Information Officers, as well as policymakers with an interest in using data as a strategic asset to enable operational excellence in government.

Please note: Registration requires a federal government or military email address. This invitation is open only to federal government employees. A government ID with a picture is required to enter the building. For questions, please contact ntisevents@ntis.gov.
