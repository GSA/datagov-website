Water Theme Adds New Content

December 8, 2014  By Emily Read

In a continued effort to provide key resources that are aimed at developing water resources resilience to climate change, five additional items are now available in the Water theme.

Those include:

  • Global Lake Temperature Collaboration
  • Forced Evaporation from Water Surface
  • Groundwater depletion in the United States (1900−2008)
  • California drought, visualized with open data
  • Federal Support Toolbox
